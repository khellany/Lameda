## Competitive Landscape

### Primary Competitors

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness vs. Lameda |
|-----------|---------|---------------------|
| WATI | WhatsApp Business API access | Not AI-native; requires manual setup |
| Bumpa | Nigerian market knowledge | E-commerce focused, not WhatsApp-native |
| ManyChat | Proven automation workflows | Not optimized for Nigerian payment rails |
| Bird.com | Enterprise WhatsApp scale | Too expensive and complex for SME fashion |
| Treble.ai | AI WhatsApp capabilities | No Nigerian market focus; USD pricing |

### Lameda's Positioning

**Primary differentiator:** The only WhatsApp AI sales assistant purpose-built for Nigerian fashion retail with local payment rails (Paystack), Naira pricing, and catalog management that understands pidgin and Nigerian product terminology.

**Positioning statement:**
"Lameda gives your WhatsApp the ability to sell 24/7 - answering product questions, capturing orders, and collecting payments automatically - so you can focus on growing your business instead of managing chat."

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Target Customer Profile

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|-------------|
| Business type | Nigerian fashion retail (clothing, accessories, footwear) |
| Size | Solo or 1-5 staff |
| WhatsApp volume | 20+ conversations per day |
| Average order value | N50,000+ |
| Tech profile | Smartphone-native; WhatsApp power user; no coding skills |
| Pain points | Lost sales from slow replies; repeat questions; payment follow-up |

### Acquisition Channels

| Channel | Priority | CAC Estimate |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Direct sales (referral-led) | Primary | N30,000 |
| Instagram and TikTok ads | Secondary | N70,000 |
| Fashion market events (Lagos) | Tertiary | N50,000 |
| Merchant referral program | Scale | N20,000 |

### Sales Motion

1. **Discovery:** Merchant sees ad or gets referral
2. **Trial signup:** 14-day free trial, no credit card
3. **Onboarding:** 25-minute guided setup (catalog upload, bot config, test run)
4. **Activation:** First real customer conversation within 48 hours
5. **Conversion:** Auto-billing at trial end; human check-in call on Day 10
6. **Expansion:** Upsell to Growth/Pro at 3-month mark based on usage

**Target conversion rate:** 80% trial-to-paid

### Launch Sequence

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| Week 1-2 | Closed beta with 5 hand-picked Lagos fashion merchants |
| Week 3-4 | Incorporate feedback, fix critical issues |
| Week 5-6 | Open beta with 20 merchants (referral only) |
| Week 7-8 | Public launch - Instagram campaign, market event |
| Month 3+ | Paid acquisition begins once trial-to-paid confirmed |
