## Business Summary

### Market Opportunity

| Level | Value | Basis |
|-------|-------|-------|
| TAM | N4.1 trillion/year | 41M Nigerian SMEs x N10K/month |
| SAM | N28.8 billion/year | 200K fashion retailers x N12K avg |
| SOM (18 months) | N90M ARR | 500 merchants at blended N15K/month |

**Why Fashion Retail:**
- High conversation volume (20+ WhatsApp chats daily per merchant)
- High average order value (N50,000+) - sufficient to absorb SaaS fee
- Low digital sophistication - merchants want WhatsApp, not a new app
- Cash-heavy operations make payment automation high-value

### Unit Economics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| ARPU (blended) | N15,000/month |
| COGS per merchant (Growth tier) | N11,636/month |
| Gross margin (early) | 21% |
| Gross margin (at scale, 1,000+ merchants) | 72% |
| LTV (18-month avg retention) | N270,000 |
| CAC (direct sales + digital) | N50,000 - N90,000 |
| LTV:CAC ratio | 3:1 to 5:1 |
| Payback period | 4-6 months |

### Revenue Model

- **Primary:** Monthly SaaS subscription (recurring)
- **Secondary (future):** Transaction fee on orders processed (0.5% at scale)
- **Tertiary (future):** Premium catalog management services

### Cost Structure (COGS per merchant, Growth tier)

| Cost Item | Monthly (NGN) |
|-----------|--------------|
| WhatsApp Business API | N3,000 |
| AI inference (Claude + OpenAI) | N5,000 |
| AWS infrastructure | N2,000 |
| Payment processing | N636 |
| **Total COGS** | **N10,636** |
| Gross margin at N15,000 | **29%** |

COGS drops sharply at scale due to AI inference bulk pricing and infrastructure amortization.

### Financial Projections

| Month | Merchants | MRR (NGN) | Status |
|-------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| 3 | 15 | N150,000 | Early traction |
| 6 | 50 | N750,000 | Growth phase |
| 9 | 150 | N2,250,000 | PMF confirmed |
| 11 | 250 | N3,750,000 | Break-even |
| 12 | 300 | N4,500,000 | Series A signal |
| 18 | 500 | N7,500,000 | Series A milestone |

### Seed Ask

**Amount:** N50,000,000 (~$30,000 USD)
**Runway:** 18 months
**Use of funds:**

| Category | Allocation | Purpose |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Engineering | 50% | 2 engineers (12 months), infrastructure |
| Sales and Marketing | 30% | Direct sales team, digital acquisition |
| Operations | 15% | Customer success, legal, compliance |
| Reserve | 5% | Buffer |

**Series A trigger:** Month 12 - 500 merchants, N7.5M MRR, clear path to N100M ARR
