# Business Model Canvas
## Lameda

### Customer Segments
- Primary: Nigerian fashion retailers using WhatsApp to sell.
- Secondary: beauty, accessories, thrift, and other social-commerce SMEs.
- Buyers: founders, operations managers, sales leads, and customer support leads.

### Value Proposition
- Turn WhatsApp and Telegram into a 24/7 sales and support assistant.
- Reduce missed sales and manual workload.
- Help merchants convert chat into payment.
- Preserve the merchant brand while Lameda operates invisibly in the background.

### Channels
- Founder direct outreach.
- Referrals and word-of-mouth.
- Instagram and WhatsApp community groups.
- Demo videos and product-led onboarding.
- Linked social content showing the bot in action.

### Customer Relationships
- 14-day free trial.
- Self-service onboarding for simple stores.
- Guided onboarding for early adopters.
- Done-for-you setup for pilots.
- In-product support and escalation.

### Revenue Streams
- Monthly subscription: ₦10,000 to ₦15,000 starting band.
- Annual plans at a discount.
- Premium onboarding or custom integration later.
- Possible transaction or usage pricing later.

### Key Activities
- Product development.
- Merchant onboarding.
- AI workflow tuning.
- Support and success management.
- Sales and partnerships.

### Key Resources
- Conversational workflow engine.
- Merchant dashboard.
- Payment integration.
- Customer and order data.
- Pilot customer relationships.

### Key Partners
- Meta messaging infrastructure.
- Paystack and bank transfer infrastructure.
- Future delivery partners.
- Cloud hosting and AI providers.

### Cost Structure
- Messaging and AI usage.
- Cloud hosting and storage.
- Support and engineering.
- Merchant acquisition and onboarding.
- Compliance and admin overhead.

### Strategic Assumptions
- The pain is severe enough to justify monthly SaaS spend.
- Merchants will accept guided onboarding.
- Product upload can be simplified through images and assisted flows.
- Customers trust chat-based commerce enough to complete payment.
