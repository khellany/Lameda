## FAQ Section

**Q: Do I need a developer to set up Lameda?**
No. Setup takes less than 25 minutes and requires no technical skills. If you can use WhatsApp, you can set up Lameda.

**Q: Do I need to change my current WhatsApp number?**
No. Lameda connects to your existing WhatsApp Business number. Your customers continue to message the same number they already know.

**Q: What happens if a customer has a complex complaint?**
Lameda automatically detects frustration and hands the conversation to you immediately with a full history. You take over in one tap.

**Q: Does it work in Nigerian Pidgin?**
Yes. You can set your AI assistant to respond in English or Nigerian Pidgin - or automatically match the customer's language.

**Q: What payment methods does it support?**
Lameda integrates with Paystack, which supports all Nigerian debit cards, bank transfers, and USSD payments.

**Q: Is my customer data safe?**
Yes. Lameda is NDPR-compliant. Your customer data is stored on servers in Nigeria. You own your data and can export or delete it at any time.

**Q: What if I have more than 100 products?**
The Growth plan supports up to 500 products and the Pro plan is unlimited. You can upgrade at any time from your dashboard.

**Q: Can I try it before paying?**
Yes. Every plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
